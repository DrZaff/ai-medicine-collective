ABOUT

The AI Medicine Collective is an interdisciplinary group of clinicians, trainees, researchers, and tech-curious learners exploring the evolving role of artificial intelligence in healthcare.

  • Practical AI tools for clinical work & education
  • Ethical and responsible implementation
  • Creative experimentation & innovation
  • Collaboration across backgrounds & training levels

Our mission is to build a community where people across medicine can learn, experiment, and innovate together — regardless of prior technical experience.
